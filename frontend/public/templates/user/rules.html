Objective

Score points by making the ball pass your opponent's paddle.

Gameplay

• The game is played by two players, each controlling a paddle on opposite sides of the screen.

• A ball moves continuously across the screen, bouncing off the top and bottom edges.

• Players move their paddles vertically to hit the ball back toward the opponent.

If a player misses the ball and it goes past their paddle, the opponent scores a point.

Scoring

Each time the ball passes the opponent's paddle, the scoring player earns 1 point.
